Goal 2: Zero Hunger

Overview

This goal aims to end hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture.

Key Targets:

  • End hunger and ensure access to safe and nutritious food.
  • End all forms of malnutrition.
  • Ensure sustainable food production systems.

Importance:

Food security is crucial for health, well-being, and development, making it essential for achieving other SDGs.
